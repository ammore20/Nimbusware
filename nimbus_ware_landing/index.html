Case Study: Torry Anchor Foods

Client: Torry Anchor Foods (TAF)

Background: Torry Anchor Foods is a premium supplier of fresh fish, chicken, and other non-vegetarian products. With a strong B2B foundation across Mumbai and Pune, they wanted to expand their footprint through a B2C digital platform, starting with hyperlocal delivery in Pimpri-Chinchwad.

Client Requirements: TAF approached Nimbus Ware with the goal of building a scalable e-commerce solution that could streamline their customer ordering experience, manage internal operations, and serve as a launchpad for future digital growth. Their key needs included:

  • A user-friendly mobile app (iOS & Android) to display products based on location and availability.
  • An engaging landing page to highlight brand values, offerings, team, and clients.
  • A robust dashboard for managing purchases, sales, credits, and profit/loss analytics with daily, weekly, and monthly filters.
  • Scope for custom delivery flow, pincode-based service restriction, and future order tracking.

Our Approach: We began with a discovery phase to deeply understand their business workflow and customer expectations. The project was broken into deliverable phases. As of now:

  • ✔️ Delivered a clean, professional landing page with dedicated sections for About Us, Products, Team, Clients, and a Case Study.
  • ✔️ Designed and implemented a comprehensive Power BI dashboard integrated with Google Sheets, offering real-time business insights and item-wise analysis.
  • ⚙️ Currently developing the cross-platform mobile app using Flutter, with features like location-based product visibility, custom pincode logic, cart flow, and secure sign-up/sign-in modules.

Collaboration & Communication: TAF’s leadership has been actively involved in shaping the direction of the platform. We maintain regular update cycles, provide progress reports, and continuously align design and functionality with their expectations.

What’s Next: We’re now focused on completing the mobile app, integrating payment gateways, order tracking features, and ensuring smooth deployment on both the Play Store and Apple App Store. Scalability and user experience remain at the core of our development roadmap.

Outcome So Far: A solid digital foundation has been laid, with strong visuals, data-driven tools, and a user-focused front end. This puts TAF in a strong position to dominate the hyperlocal meat delivery space in their region upon full launch.
